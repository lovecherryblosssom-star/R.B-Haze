Our Story

Lahore's Favourite
Artisan Dessert Boutique

Rose Berry Haze was born from a simple belief — that every dessert should feel like a celebration. From our rose-kissed pastries to our signature berry glazed tarts, every creation is handcrafted in small batches using the finest ingredients, with no shortcuts and no compromise on quality.

We are proud to be Lahore's go-to destination for bespoke cakes, gifting boxes, and seasonal confections — trusted by thousands of families and counted among the city's most loved sweet boutiques.
